## How to read this pack

- Each requirement area has its own evidence table. Every row maps one requirement to an Azure capability with a verdict, evidence, status, source link, and verification date.
- **Supported** is `Yes`, `Partial`, or `No`. Partial and No rows are intentional RFP evidence — read `Gaps & caveats`.
- Cells marked `[TBD - needs verification]`, `[TBD - needs subscription]`, or `[TBD - ask customer]` are open items, listed again under Open questions.
- Facts are grounded in Microsoft Learn at the verification date. Exact per-token prices and per-region/per-model quota are live values — confirm on the Azure pricing page and in your connected subscription before any commitment.
- This pack is point-in-time; re-verify before contractual commitments.
